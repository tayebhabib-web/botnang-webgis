Scientific scenario calculation

Annual Growth Rate = (Population_t / Population_t-1) - 1 Baseline = average annual historical growth = -0.0700% per year Standard deviation = 0.8748% per year Growth = average + standard deviation = 0.8048% per year Decline = average - standard deviation = -0.9448% per year Future Population = Population_2025 × (1 + Scenario Rate)^(Forecast Year - 2025)
